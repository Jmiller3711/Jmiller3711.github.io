Why do I Brew?

It's more than just the end product. Brewing beer as a hobby has given me an appreciation for all styles of beer, allows me to experiment, and gives me a great sense of pride.

Brewing History

My first brew was in a small 5 gallon kettle atop my apartment stove in downtown KC. Not knowing what I was getting into, I started my first brew at 8pm and finsihed somwhere around 2am, which resulted in a long day at work the following day. Check out my brewing history.

Brew Schedule

I have a yearly goal to brew at least eight times a year. This should keep my tap handels wet year roung. As of 2017, I decided to abide by a brewing schedule that consists of four "staple" beers: Irish Red, Fruity Blonde, Pumpkin Porter, and Coffee Stout. Each year, I try to improve upon last years recipie.
